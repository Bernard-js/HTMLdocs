Displaying video text tracks

Introduction to WebVTT

some people, for many reasons that will be discussed later, will need to read text transcriptions of the audio tracks in some video tracks. Thanks to HTML5, it's possible to add cues to video files, such as:

  • Subtitles
  • Captions
  • Timed descriptions

to do so, we use a file format called WebVTT, in which the captions will be at. then, we use a <track> element, as a child of the video element and after the source element, with a src attribute referencing to the .vtt file. there are other attributes, such as kind and srclang that will be discussed later.
